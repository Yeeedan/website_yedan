Typeface
</br>Intership in 3type (TypeSchool),</br>Summer 2025

While living in central Shanghai, I frequently passed luxury boutiques and noticed that many brands utilized high-contrast, wide-set typefaces. I was drawn to the stability and expansiveness of these forms—they project a certain confidence that comes from occupying space. 
However, I found them lacking a sense of "vivant".  So I created Ample, inspired by the amphitheater, like a gap in city life. It's also a revival based on Ollière & Cie's specimen. This practice improved my ability to rapidly build a foundational type system in two days...
